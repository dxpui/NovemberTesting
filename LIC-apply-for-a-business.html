Apply for a business radio licence

Spectrum Radio equipment Licence information
Published: 9 October 2023
Last updated: 5 December 2024

If you use a radio system for your business then you will need a licence from Ofcom. Business radio users range from taxi companies and factories, to hospitals, care homes, industrial sites and transport operators.

Apply online for a new business radio licence

You can use our Online Licensing Service to apply for any Business Radio licence product quickly and easily.

We are making additional non-contiguous spectrum available in the UHF2 band (from 453 MHz to 466 MHz) for licences up to six months resulting from negotiations with the Emergency Services. Technically Assigned licences will be available until April 2022. There is no provision for Area Defined licences. The additional spectrum will be used to cater for simplex assignments primarily although requests for duplex assignments will be considered. If you require further information please contact  spectrum.licensing@ofcom.org.uk.

You can also apply by downloading a form and sending it to our office.
